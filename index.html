About

The Heavy Equipment Industries Interest Group of the American Statistical Association (ASA) provides a professional forum for statisticians, engineers, and data scientists working in large-scale, capital-intensive, and engineering-driven manufacturing sectors.

These sectors include industries such as shipbuilding, semiconductor and memory chip fabrication, aerospace, construction equipment, and energy systems, where production involves complex multistage processes, high-precision operations, and long lifecycle performance.

Purpose

The purpose of this Interest Group is to advance the application of statistical science in heavy equipment and advanced manufacturing industries. The group promotes collaboration between academia and industry to address statistical challenges arising in complex engineering systems.

Scope

  • Promote statistical thinking and methodology in heavy equipment and advanced manufacturing industries.
  • Encourage research on multistage and complex manufacturing processes.
  • Support innovation in reliability, lifecycle performance, and predictive maintenance.
  • Promote integration of statistics with smart manufacturing, digital twins, and AI-driven industrial systems.
  • Facilitate collaboration among statisticians, engineers, and industry practitioners.
